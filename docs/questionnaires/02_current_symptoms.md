# Current Symptom Inventory

> Go system by system. For each symptom: when it started, how often, how severe (1-10),
> what makes it better/worse, and whether it's gotten worse over time.

## Energy & Fatigue

- On a typical day, how would you rate your energy 1-10?
- Is there a pattern? Worse in morning vs evening?
- Do you crash after meals? After exercise? After mental effort?
- Can you push through fatigue or does it physically stop you?
- Post-exertional malaise? (feeling worse 12-48h after exertion)
- Does your fatigue feel more like "sleepiness" or "cellular depletion" — like your muscles just won't produce energy? (relevant if you have mitochondrial gene variants)
- How do you respond to acute stress? Do you get an adrenaline response, or does stress just flatten you?

## Sleep

- How many hours do you typically sleep?
- How long to fall asleep?
- Do you wake during the night? How often? Can you fall back asleep?
- What time do you naturally want to sleep and wake? (chronotype)
- Do you feel refreshed after sleep, ever?
- Vivid dreams? Night sweats? Restless legs?
- Snoring or breathing issues?
- What have you tried for sleep? What worked, what didn't?

## Autonomic / Dysautonomia

- Lightheadedness on standing?
- Heart racing when standing or after meals?
- Exercise intolerance — can you run? Climb stairs? At what point does your heart rate go too high?
- Temperature regulation — do you overheat easily? Cold hands/feet?
- Sweating patterns — too much? Too little? Asymmetric?
- Blood pressure — do you know your typical readings?
- Do you faint or come close to fainting?

## Neurological

- Brain fog — difficulty concentrating, word-finding, processing speed?
- Headaches — frequency, type, location, severity?
- Migraines with aura?
- Tremor?
- Numbness or tingling — where?
- Vision changes — blurring, floaters, light sensitivity?
- Tinnitus?
- Dizziness vs vertigo (room spinning)?

## Psychiatric / Mood

- How would you describe your baseline mood?
- Depression — current or past episodes? Duration? Severity?
- Anxiety — generalized? Social? Panic attacks?
- Mood cycling — do you have periods of high energy/productivity alternating with crashes?
- Irritability — is it a consistent problem?
- Dissociation — do you ever feel "not real" or detached from your body?
- Suicidal ideation — past or present? (important for clinical picture)
- Obsessive patterns — rumination, checking, perfectionism?

## GI

- Bloating, gas, cramping — how often?
- Bowel habits — constipation, diarrhea, alternating?
- Food intolerances? Any reactions to gluten specifically?
- Nausea?
- Reflux/heartburn?
- Have you ever been evaluated for celiac, SIBO, or IBD?

## Musculoskeletal

- Joint pain — which joints? Stiffness in the morning?
- Back pain — history?
- Hypermobility — can you hyperextend elbows, knees, thumbs? (relevant to dysautonomia)
- Muscle pain or cramping?

## Skin

- Any rashes or skin conditions? (eczema, psoriasis, dermatitis)
- Easy bruising?
- Wound healing — normal or slow?
- Stretch marks?
- Skin flushing — triggers?

## Cardiovascular

- Chest pain or pressure?
- Palpitations — how often?
- Known heart murmur?
- Any echocardiogram or ECG findings?

## Respiratory

- Shortness of breath — at rest? On exertion?
- Chronic cough?
- Asthma?

## Inflammatory / Immune Pattern

If your labs show immune dysregulation (elevated cytokines, abnormal cortisol, rising ferritin, etc.):

- Do you get sick (colds, infections) more or less often than others?
- When you get sick, does it take longer than normal to recover?
- Do you notice any cyclical pattern to symptoms — worse some weeks, better others? Any correlation with stress, sleep, or diet?
- Any swollen lymph nodes, especially during flares?
- Do anti-inflammatory things (NSAIDs, turmeric, fish oil) noticeably help your fatigue or brain fog?
- Have you ever been told you have elevated inflammatory markers by a doctor?
