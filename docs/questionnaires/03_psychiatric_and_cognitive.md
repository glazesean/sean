# Psychiatric & Cognitive Deep Dive

> If your genomic profile shows elevated psychiatric PRS scores,
> understanding your actual psychiatric phenotype is critical for interpretation.
> High PRS doesn't mean you have these conditions — but anchoring against
> reality is essential.

## Cognitive Function

- What's your subjective cognitive ability? Areas of strength vs weakness?
- Has your cognitive performance changed over time? Sharper, duller, or different?
- Memory — short-term vs long-term, any concerns?
- Processing speed — do you feel "slow" sometimes?
- Executive function — planning, organizing, starting tasks, switching between tasks?
- Do you consider yourself neurodivergent in any way?

## Mood & Affect

- If you had to describe your "emotional operating system" — what's the default state?
- Do you experience emotional extremes? How intense?
- Anhedonia — do you lose the ability to feel pleasure? When?
- How do you handle stress? What's your breaking point?
- Do people in your life describe your mood as stable or variable?

## Cyclothymic / Hypomanic Patterns

- Do you recognize periods of elevated energy, reduced sleep need, increased productivity, grandiosity, racing thoughts, increased goal-directed activity?
- How long do these last? Days? Weeks?
- What's the crash like after?
- Have these episodes ever caused problems (financial, relational, professional)?
- Has anyone ever suggested bipolar spectrum?

## Dissociation

- Depersonalization — feeling detached from your body, like you're watching yourself?
- Derealization — the world feeling "unreal" or dreamlike?
- Gaps in memory for certain periods?
- How frequent? Triggers?

## Anxiety

- What does your anxiety feel like? Physical symptoms?
- Panic attacks — ever? Frequency? What triggers them?
- Social anxiety — avoidance patterns?
- Health anxiety — does health research feed into anxiety loops?
- OCD-spectrum — intrusive thoughts, compulsions, rituals?

## Substance Use

- Alcohol — frequency, quantity, any binge patterns?
- Cannabis — frequency?
- Caffeine — how much? How does it affect you?
- Nicotine — ever?
- Stimulants — ever used for focus/productivity?
- Psychedelics — any experience? Effects?
- Any substance you've felt dependent on?

## Interpersonal

- Relationship patterns — any recurring issues?
- Do you find it hard to maintain close relationships?
- Emotional regulation in conflict — what happens?
- Isolation vs connection — what's your natural tendency?

## Treatment History

- Any psychotherapy? What type? (CBT, psychodynamic, EMDR, etc.) How long? Helpful?
- Any psychiatric medications? (SSRIs, SNRIs, mood stabilizers, anxiolytics)
- What has actually helped your mental health? What made it worse?
- What's your current approach to mental health management?

## Trauma History (only share what you're comfortable with)

- Any significant adverse childhood experiences?
- Any traumatic events in adulthood?
- If your cortisol diurnal profile is abnormal (flattened, absent CAR), that pattern is consistent with chronic stress exposure — does that match your history?
