# Lifestyle & Environment

## Diet

- How would you describe your typical diet? Any structure or philosophy?
- Do you track macros/calories?
- Protein intake — rough daily grams?
- Any food intolerances? How do you manage them?
- Fasting — IF or extended? What pattern?
- Any foods you've noticed make you feel better or worse?
- Hydration — how much water/fluids daily?
- Salt intake — relevant if you have dysautonomia (high salt often recommended)

## Exercise

- What does a typical week of exercise look like?
- What types of exercise? (strength, cardio, walking, etc.)
- Can you do high-intensity exercise? What limits you?
- Heart rate during exercise — do you monitor?
- Post-exercise — how do you feel? Recovery time?
- Has your exercise capacity changed over time?
- Any periods of being very fit vs very sedentary?

## Body Composition

- Current weight, height, estimated body fat %?
- Have you had significant weight fluctuations? When and why?
- Is your current weight your natural setpoint or actively maintained?

## Sleep Hygiene

- What does your bedtime routine look like?
- Screen habits before bed?
- Room temperature, darkness, noise?
- Sleep aids — melatonin, magnesium, prescription, supplements?
- Naps — do you take them? How do they affect nighttime sleep?
- How does your sleep change with travel/time zones?

## Substances & Supplements

- **Current supplement stack** — list everything with doses and timing
- Why each supplement? What you've noticed?
- Any supplements you've tried and dropped? Why?
- If you have relevant gene variants, note responses to targeted supplements:
  - Mitochondrial support (CoQ10, ubiquinol, PQQ)
  - NAD+ precursors (NMN, NR, niacin)
  - Anti-inflammatory (curcumin, omega-3)
  - Methylation support (methylfolate, B-complex)
  - Vitamin D3+K2
- Alcohol — how many drinks per week typically?
- Cannabis — frequency?
- Caffeine — type, amount, timing?

## Environment & Exposures

- Where do you spend most of your time? (home, office, coworking)
- Air quality concerns?
- Mold exposure — past or current?
- Chemical exposures — anything unusual?
- Sun exposure — how much? SPF use?

## Stress & Work

- What do you do for work?
- Work hours per week?
- Work-related stress level 1-10?
- What's your biggest source of stress right now?
- Do you have periods of intense productivity followed by burnout?

## Social & Relationships

- Living situation?
- Close relationships — partner, friends, family nearby?
- Social activity level — how often do you see people?

## Geographic History

- Where have you lived? Any moves that correlated with health changes?
- Travel frequency?
