# Family & Medical History

> Answer these however feels natural — rambling is fine, the AI will extract the structure.
> Focus on what you actually know vs. what you're guessing.

## Family History

**Parents:**
- Are both parents alive? Ages? General health?
- Any chronic conditions (diabetes, heart disease, autoimmune, cancer, psychiatric)?
- Any of them on long-term medications?
- Did either parent have issues with sleep, mood, energy, pain, or GI problems?

**Siblings:**
- Any siblings? Ages? Shared health patterns?
- Any siblings with sleep disorders, fatigue, or similar symptoms?

**Extended family (grandparents, aunts, uncles):**
- Any early deaths (<65)? Cause?
- Cancer — which types, what ages?
- Heart attacks or strokes — what ages?
- Psychiatric illness — depression, bipolar, schizophrenia, substance abuse, suicide?
- Autoimmune diseases — thyroid, lupus, MS, celiac, IBD?
- Neurodegenerative — Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, ALS?
- Chronic fatigue, fibromyalgia, or "mystery illness" in any relatives?

**If you have rare variants from genomic testing, add targeted questions here:**

**Why family history matters genetically:** PRS clusters suggest genetic loading — family history tells you whether this has penetrated phenotypically across generations or whether you're a de novo accumulation. Rare variants need parental context to determine clinical urgency (inherited vs de novo, penetrance).

## Your Medical Timeline

**Childhood & adolescence (0-18):**
- Any developmental concerns? (speech, motor, social milestones)
- Any chronic illnesses, hospitalizations, or surgeries as a child?
- When did any chronic symptoms (insomnia, fatigue, pain) first start?
- Any significant medical events or diagnoses?

**Early adulthood (18-30):**
- First time you sought medical help for something beyond routine?
- When did current health issues start?
- Any psychiatric diagnoses or treatment? Therapy? Medications tried?
- Any injuries or surgeries?

**Recent history:**
- Walk through major medical events and evaluations
- Any specialist evaluations? What prompted them? What were conclusions?
- Any infectious triggers for current symptoms? (post-viral illness is relevant for dysautonomia)

## Medication History

- **Current medications/supplements** — everything you take daily, with doses
- **Past medications** — anything you've tried and stopped, and why you stopped
- **Any psychiatric medications** ever tried? SSRIs, SNRIs, stimulants, mood stabilizers, benzos, antipsychotics?
- **Drug reactions** — anything that worked unusually well or unusually badly?
- **If you know your PGx profile** — have you noticed anything with drugs metabolized by your variant CYP enzymes?

## Surgeries & Hospitalizations

- Any surgeries? (even minor — dental, wisdom teeth, moles)
- Any general anesthesia? Any unusual reactions?
- Any ER visits or hospitalizations?

## Allergies

- Drug allergies?
- Food allergies/intolerances?
- Environmental allergies? (pollen, dust, pets)
- Any history of allergic reactions or anaphylaxis?
